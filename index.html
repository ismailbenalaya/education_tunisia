Quick FAQ

Why are student scores declining?

Despite high access, quality has suffered due to outdated curricula, lack of teacher support, and infrastructure issues.

What is the biggest challenge?

A "severe learning crisis" where enrollment expansion hasn't been matched by learning outcomes, exacerbated by regional inequality.
